SEATING TIMES

1st matinee: Seating begins 30 minutes prior to the start of the movie.

2nd matinee: Seating begins 45 minutes prior to the start of the movie.

Late movie: Seating begins 30 minutes prior to the start of the movie.

Keep in mind, food (burgers, sandwiches, salads, etc.) is delivered only to the balcony seats. Just order at concessions, grab a seat in the balcony, and we'll bring it to you! *It is recommended that you find your balcony seats before coming down to order on busy/sold out shows.
